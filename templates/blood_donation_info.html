The Importance of Blood Donation

Blood donation is a vital process that saves millions of lives every year. Donated blood is used in surgeries, trauma care, cancer treatment, and for patients with chronic illnesses. By donating blood, you are giving someone a second chance at life.

How You Can Contribute

  • Register as a blood donor and keep your profile updated.
  • Participate in blood donation drives organized by your community or institution.
  • Encourage friends and family to donate blood regularly.
  • Spread awareness about the need for safe and voluntary blood donation.

Who Can Donate?

  • Healthy individuals aged 18-65 years.
  • Minimum weight of 50 kg.
  • No major illnesses or recent infections.
  • Meet the eligibility criteria set by health authorities.

Your contribution can make a difference. Donate blood, save lives!
